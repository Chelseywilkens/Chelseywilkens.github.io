About

Chelsey Wilkens

As a personal trainer, wellness writer, holistic nutritionist and former gymnast, Chelsey believes that your life is about creating balance. As you explore the world and become educated about the best ways to take care of yourself, you become in-tune with what truly works for you. “Exercise should be a way to connect your self to yourself. Understanding how your body functions, getting it to a place where you move without pain or resistance and are able to use your body as a tool for health, movement, and aesthetic desire. This is why I love being a coach!”. We all go out of balance, Wilko Journal is here as a healthy reminder of how you can travel, work hard, and take care of yourself with PASSION!
